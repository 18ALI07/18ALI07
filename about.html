Making the Planet Safer and Greener with yourgreen

We at Nelsun Coir strive hard to make a change, to make the world a better and healthier place for everyone. Nelsun Coir is a Tuticorin-based private limited company specialising in producing coir pith which is extracted from the coconut husk. Coir pith has a variety of applications and they are 100% eco-friendly.

The world needs saving, our activities have degraded the nature and her beauty. The air is polluted; quality of water resources have degraded and the land is getting eroded. Nature has given us everything but we have disrespected her integrity by exploiting her gifts pointlessly. The human activities have distorted the ecological balance and if action is not taken immediately then we will not be able to give our children a beautiful tomorrow.

Coir pith is generally used as a growing medium or a soil substitute and a soil conditioner. They are also used in potting mix in landscaping, floriculture, horticulture and domestic gardens. We always manufacture best quality products and this unparalleled quality of our products and exceptional customer service helped us gain an international stature.

We export our products to the Nederlands, USA, Spain, UK, Canada, Korea, Ireland, Australia, Israel and UAE, parts of the USA, Mexico, Canada, Holland, Spain, UK, Israel, UAE, Australia and New Zealand

Our products are manufactured in our own production houses located across the state of Tamil Nadu and this gives us full control over the raw materials thereby helping us to ensure the highest quality and in-time delivery of our products.

It has been over a decade since we started our journey in 1998 and in all these years we have produced much innovative value added products through extensive research but still we have a long way to go to ensure a healthier and safer tomorrow for our kids to breathe in.
